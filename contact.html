Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and organization.

You can apply through our online application form on the Programs page. We accept applications on a rolling basis for most programs, with specific deadlines for cohort-based programs like Emerging Leaders.

Our programs primarily serve young people aged 16-25, with some programs extending to 30. We believe leadership development can start early and continue throughout young adulthood.

Many of our programs are offered at no cost to participants thanks to our generous partners and donors. Some intensive programs have nominal fees, but we provide full scholarships to ensure accessibility for all.

We welcome partnerships with organizations of all sizes. Partnership opportunities include program funding, mentorship, venue sponsorship, and collaborative community projects. Contact us to discuss how we can work together.

Yes! We offer hybrid and fully virtual options for most programs. Our virtual programs maintain the same high-quality content and community engagement through interactive online platforms.

We use a comprehensive impact measurement framework that tracks participant outcomes, community impact, and long-term leadership development. We publish annual impact reports and share results with our community.
