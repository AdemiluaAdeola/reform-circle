About The Reform Circle

We're on a mission to empower the next generation of leaders through innovative programs, meaningful advocacy, and transformative community impact.

Our Mission

We believe that every young person has the potential to be a leader and create meaningful change in their communities. Our mission is to unlock that potential through comprehensive leadership development, advocacy training, and mentorship programs.

By combining practical skills development with real-world application, we prepare young leaders to tackle the complex challenges facing their communities and drive sustainable, positive change.

A world where young leaders are equipped with the skills, confidence, and resources to create positive change in their communities and beyond.

Our Values

The core principles that guide our work and shape our approach to youth leadership development.

Authentic Leadership

We believe in developing leaders who are true to themselves and their values, creating genuine impact through authentic connections and actions.

Inclusive Excellence

We embrace diversity in all its forms and create inclusive spaces where every young person can thrive and contribute their unique perspectives and talents.

Measurable Impact

We are committed to data-driven approaches and continuous improvement, ensuring our programs create real, measurable change in communities.

Community-Centered

We work hand-in-hand with communities to understand their needs and co-create solutions that are culturally relevant and sustainably impactful.

Continuous Learning

We foster a culture of curiosity and growth, constantly seeking new knowledge and approaches to better serve our mission and the young leaders we support.

Radical Empathy

We lead with empathy in all our interactions, seeking to understand different perspectives and create bridges between diverse communities and stakeholders.
