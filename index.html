About The Reform Circle

A youth-led movement focused on leadership, advocacy, and social impact.

Building Leaders Who Drive Change

We're building a community of young people who don't just talk about change — they drive it. Our goal is to help students and youth understand how governance works, how decisions are made, and how much influence they actually have when they step up with purpose.

We kicked off with The G.A.M.E Seminar, where we brought together policymakers, student leaders, and change-driven youths to spark conversations on leadership and civic participation.

Now, we're stepping into a new phase — one focused on consistent engagement, real community action, and honest conversations about leadership and reform.

Our Mission

To empower young people with the knowledge, skills, and platform to actively participate in governance and drive meaningful social change.

Our Vision

A generation of informed, inspired, and ready-to-lead youth actively shaping the future of their communities and nation.
